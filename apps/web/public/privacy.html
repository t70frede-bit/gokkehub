Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

GokkeHub ("we", "us") operates the games and services available at gokkehub.com and its subdomains. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

When you sign in with Discord we receive your Discord user ID, username, and email address. When you connect Spotify we receive your Spotify user ID, display name, and OAuth tokens needed to play music. When you connect Steam we receive your Steam ID and public profile information.

We store a session identifier in an HttpOnly cookie so you stay logged in across games.

How we use it

All data collected is used exclusively to operate the games on GokkeHub. Your Discord identity is used to identify you in lobbies. Your Spotify credentials are used solely to stream music during gameplay. Your Steam library is used to generate game-based challenges.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. We do not use your data for advertising or profiling.

Data storage

Session data is stored in Cloudflare KV with a 7-day expiry. OAuth tokens are stored server-side only and are never exposed to the browser. Profile information is stored in a Supabase (PostgreSQL) database hosted in the EU.

Third-party services

We use Discord, Spotify, and Steam OAuth for authentication. Their own privacy policies apply when you interact with their platforms.

Deleting your data

You can disconnect any linked account from your profile page at any time. To request full deletion of your account data, contact us via Discord.

Contact

Questions? Find us on Discord or reach out through gokkehub.com.
